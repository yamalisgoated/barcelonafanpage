FC Barcelona Squad (2024–2025)

  • Goalkeepers:

  • Marc-André ter Stegen – First-choice keeper, sidelined due to injury

  • Iñaki Peña – Stepping up as the current starter

  • Wojciech Szczęsny – Signed in Oct 2024, standout in Copa del Rey & Champions League

  • Defenders:

  • Ronald Araújo – Defensive leader and key center-back

  • Jules Koundé – Versatile defender, CB and RB

  • Andreas Christensen – Reliable and composed central defender

  • Alejandro Balde – Dynamic and youthful left-back

  • Jonathan Tah – New signing from Leverkusen, adds experience

  • Eric García – Returned from Girona loan; praised by Hansi Flick

  • Midfielders:

  • Frenkie de Jong – Key to ball progression and control

  • Pedri – Creative playmaker

  • Gavi – Energetic La Masia graduate

  • Dani Olmo – Returned in Aug 2024, impactful in La Liga & UCL

  • Oriol Romeu – Adds depth and experience

  • Forwards:

  • Robert Lewandowski – Prolific striker

  • Raphinha – Fast and creative Brazilian winger

  • Lamine Yamal – Young La Masia gem

  • Ferran Torres – Versatile across attack

  • Pau Víctor – Signed from Girona for €2.45M; rising talent

  • Manager:

  • Hansi Flick – In May 2024, Flick took over as the head coach of FC Barcelona, succeeding Xavi Hernández. He signed a contract extending until June 2026, with the objective of revitalizing the team's performance both domestically and in European competitions.​
